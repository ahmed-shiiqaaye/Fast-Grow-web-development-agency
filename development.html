Our development expertise encompasses:

  • Cloud-based apps
  • Custom web app development

We provide customised solutions for your business – no matter your need, we have the team and technology to assist.

the application development process

company overview

We begin by meeting with your team, carefully documenting and discussing your project vision and needs. This time allows us to formulate a clear understanding of the scope and allows you to meet with the team that you'll be working closely with during the course of your application development project.

consulting scope

Once our team has familiarised themselves with your project, we conduct research on our end that allows us to expertly consult you on a project solution. This includes a complete project development timeframe, costing, scope and technological requirements.

evaluation

Once the initial project development is completed on our side, it is handed over to your team and you’re allowed time to access the outcome of the work. Thorough testing is conducted both from our side and yours at this point. We’re open to receiving client insight and feedback to further improve the project

launch

Once the project has met your expectation (& ours). We conduct further internal and external testing to ensure that the project is perfected without any glitches and that the solution has been perfected. The project is then launched. Our team is always on standby after launch to ensure that things remain running smoothly.
